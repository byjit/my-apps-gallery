# TrueRVU

**Recover lost money due to under-reported RVUs.**

Made for physicians who are paid based on wRVUs. Pick your five most-billed CPT codes, tap to log each encounter, then upload your paystub — TrueRVU reads the reported wRVU total and shows the exact shortfall in dollars, with a dispute-ready CSV breakdown.

Free forever for unlimited logging; Pro adds automated paystub review at $9/mo.
